The City

The city holds some interesting views.

The Summit One Vanderbilt experience. An amazing view.

The Summit One Vanderbilt starts with an amazing elevator ride showing the true scale of the building's height. You are then placed in a mirror room that offers a breathtaking view of Manhattan.

The Smithsonian. A blast to the past.

The building's architecture alone catches your eye before you enter the museum. Once you enter, you are greeted with many areas to explore. From ancient artifacts to fossils. There is so much to see in so little time. My personal favorite part of the trip.

The city. Always brimming with activity.

There is always something to do in city. From going to central park or eating at numerous restaurants, the city offers something for everyone.
